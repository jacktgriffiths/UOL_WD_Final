An introduction to Double Jaw Surgery...

Welcome to the wonderful world of Double Jaw Surgery. This website has been put together as an online magazine containing infomation and first hand experiences related to all things jaw surgery. Whether you are looking for information about jaw surgery, or you are looking to submit your own stories and exepriences, this is the place to be. If this is your first introduction to jaw surgery (or double jaw surgery in particular) the video to the right gives a quick explination of the surgical process.

I hope you enjoy looking around and find the website informative. If you think there is anything missing, or have any feedback, please use the contact form on the 'Learn More' page.

Thank you and I hope you enjoy.
